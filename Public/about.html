About Us

The Islamic Centre of Nanaimo serves the Mid-Vancouver Island region—from Duncan in the south to Tofino in the west and Port Hardy in the north. We are a hub for spiritual growth, education, and community engagement.

Our mission is to promote Islamic values of peace, compassion, and unity while providing a welcoming space for worship, education, and community events.

Location: 897 Harbourview Street, Nanaimo, BC

Our History

Early Beginnings (1970-1989)

Muslims have been living in Nanaimo since at least 1970. The first organized Muslim community in Nanaimo was established in 1989 by two dedicated individuals. At that time, the community consisted of only a few families who began by celebrating important Islamic occasions. Soon after, regular Friday prayers were initiated, hosted on a rotation basis at different family homes.

Growth and Organization (1997-2000)

In 1997, the Islamic community of Nanaimo joined the Vancouver Island Muslim Association, based in Victoria. Two years later, in 1999, the Vancouver Island Muslim Association became a chapter of the B.C. Muslim Association (BCMA).

With the support of both the local community and the BCMA, a major milestone was reached in July 1999 when a building was purchased for use as an Islamic Centre. The building, originally constructed over four decades earlier as a Chinese church in Nanaimo's historic Chinatown and later used as a Francophone school by L'Association des Francophones de Nanaimo, was officially opened as the Islamic Centre of Nanaimo on July 15, 2000. The opening ceremony was attended by high-ranking city officials and members of the public and received significant coverage in local media.

Expansion (2017)

As the Muslim population in Nanaimo and surrounding areas grew, the original centre became insufficient to accommodate congregants for Friday and Eid prayers. Thanks to generous donations from the community, a larger building was purchased in 2017 and converted into a facility designed to meet the evolving needs of the community.

Today

The Nanaimo Chapter of the BCMA, operating from the Islamic Centre of Nanaimo at 897 Harbourview Street, serves the Mid-Vancouver Island region—from Duncan in the south to Tofino in the west and Port Hardy in the north.

We continue to provide spiritual guidance, educational programs, and community services to Muslims and the wider community throughout Vancouver Island.
