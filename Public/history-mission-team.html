Our History

The Islamic Centre of Nanaimo was established in 2005 to serve the growing Muslim community in Nanaimo. Over the years, we have become a hub for spiritual growth, education, and community engagement.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote Islamic values of peace, compassion, and unity while providing a welcoming space for worship, education, and community events.

Our Team

Our team is composed of dedicated volunteers and staff who work tirelessly to support the community. From organizing events to maintaining the mosque, we are committed to serving you.
