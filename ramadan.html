Ramadan

Ramadan Calendar and Taraweeh Information

See the Ramadan calendar, daily juz schedule, and taraweeh details.

Nightly order

Isha adhan is followed by a short reminder, then taraweeh begins. Confirm exact local times nightly with the centre.

Prayer format

Taraweeh is listed here as 20 rak'ah prayed in sets of two, followed by witr.

Khutbah and reminders

Friday khutbah remains on the Friday Prayer page. A short nightly bayan or reminder can be delivered before taraweeh.
